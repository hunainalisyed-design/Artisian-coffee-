Our Story

From Bean to Cup

Founded in 2018, Ember & Grain started as a small passion project. Today, we continue to honor the craft of coffee-making, sourcing beans directly from farmers who share our values of sustainability and quality.

Our pastries are baked fresh every morning by our team of skilled bakers, using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.
